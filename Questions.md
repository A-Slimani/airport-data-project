Operational Efficiency:
    What's the average delay time by airline, route, or time of day? 
    Which airlines have the best on-time performance? 
    How do delays cascade through the day (does a morning delay affect evening flights)?
    What's the turnaround time for aircraft at different gates/terminals?

Capacity & Planning:

    What are the peak vs off-peak hours/days for arrivals and departures?
    Which routes have the highest frequency of flights?
    How efficiently are gates being utilized throughout the day?
    What's the average time between flights at each gate?

Predictive Analytics:

    Can you predict flight delays based on time of day, airline, weather, or historical patterns?
    What factors correlate most strongly with cancellations?
    Can you forecast daily passenger volume based on flight schedules?

Network Analysis:

    Which destinations have the most direct connections?
    What are the most common connection patterns (hub analysis)?
    Which airlines dominate which routes or terminals?

Comparative Analysis:

    How does Sydney compare to other major airports (if you add more data sources)?
    Seasonal trends - busiest months, holiday patterns
    International vs domestic flight patterns

Data Quality Projects:

    Build a monitoring dashboard for data freshness and completeness
    Create anomaly detection for unusual flight patterns
    Track and report on missing or inconsistent data fields

    Historical performance metrics by airline
